Code of Ethics

吉萝卜S.r.l. has adopted this Code of Ethics, which applies to all its operations, both in Italy and abroad, and to all its employees, directors, and collaborators. The Code is based on the principles of integrity, transparency, and respect for human rights.

1. Integrity and Honesty

All employees, directors, and collaborators must act with integrity and honesty in all their business dealings. They must avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to the company.

2. Respect for Human Rights

The company respects and promotes human rights as defined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. It prohibits all forms of discrimination, harassment, and exploitation.

3. Compliance with Laws

The company complies with all applicable laws and regulations, including those related to anti-corruption, labor, environment, and data protection.

4. Responsibility towards Stakeholders

The company is responsible towards its stakeholders, including customers, employees, suppliers, and the communities in which it operates. It strives to create value for all stakeholders.

5. Environmental Responsibility

The company is committed to minimizing its environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices.

6. Reporting and Compliance

The company has established a reporting mechanism for employees and other stakeholders to report suspected violations of this Code. It investigates all reports promptly and thoroughly.
