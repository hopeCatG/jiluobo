Social Responsibility Policy

吉萝卜S.r.l. has adopted this Social Responsibility Policy, which applies to all its operations, both in Italy and abroad, and to all its employees, directors, and collaborators. The Policy is based on the principles of integrity, transparency, and respect for human rights, and it is consistent with the company's Code of Ethics.

1. Respect for Human Rights

The company is committed to respecting and promoting human rights as defined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and other international instruments. It prohibits all forms of discrimination, harassment, and exploitation, and it ensures that its operations do not contribute to human rights violations.

2. Labor Practices

The company is committed to providing safe and healthy working conditions, fair wages, and equal opportunities for all employees. It respects the right to freedom of association and collective bargaining, and it prohibits child labor and forced labor.

3. Community Engagement

The company is committed to contributing to the sustainable development of the communities in which it operates. It supports local initiatives and projects that promote education, health, and environmental protection.

4. Supply Chain Management

The company is committed to ensuring that its suppliers and business partners adhere to the same social responsibility standards. It conducts regular audits and assessments to verify compliance.

5. Implementation and Monitoring

The company has established a system for implementing and monitoring this Policy. It provides training to all employees and collaborators, and it regularly reviews and updates the Policy to ensure its effectiveness.
