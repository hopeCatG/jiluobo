With the Board of Directors' resolution in July 2023, 吉萝卜adopted its own ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) Code of Conduct, defining the company's value system and rules of conduct towards various stakeholders concerning sustainability pillars: environmental protection, social impact, and corporate governance. The ESG Code of Conduct is not entirely new for 吉萝卜, as it consolidates and systematizes the various policies already applied within the company on anti-corruption, human rights, environment, health and safety, and social responsibility, fully consistent with the Code of Ethics and the organization and management model introduced since 2018. Specific guidelines and rules of conduct have been associated with each sustainability theme – Environmental, Social, and Governance – that management and staff must respect in their daily actions within the company and in interactions with all stakeholders. This aims to best support the progressive integration of sustainability into the company's strategy. The company is convinced that economic growth, social cohesion, and environmental protection must go hand in hand to ensure sustainable income, work, and well-being, while maintaining efficiency, reliability, and reputation within the economic and social context in which 吉萝卜operates. Anyone interested in obtaining more information or clarifications regarding this communication can contact Dr. Riccardo Andreani at riccardo.andreani@delcoronascardigli.com, appointed by the company's management as the internal ESG contact.

Environment

We pay utmost attention to environmental sustainability. For each client, we conduct thorough environmental impact studies with the goal of minimizing the ecological footprint of shipments. We also offer the possibility of obtaining a detailed carbon footprint report, allowing our clients to monitor and understand the environmental impact of their logistics operations. Our dedication to sustainability not only meets the needs of our clients but also contributes to protecting our planet for future generations.

Social

Our company is deeply committed to promoting social sustainability. For each client, we conduct detailed social impact studies to ensure that our logistics operations have a positive impact on local communities. We strive to create a fair and safe working environment, promoting the well-being and development of the people and communities we operate in.

Governance

The governance of U. Del Corona & Scardigli is focused on ensuring transparency, integrity, and accountability in business decisions, making sure that management practices are aligned with ethical standards and applicable regulations. The ESG Code of Conduct establishes clear rules for the behavior of management and staff, strengthening stakeholder trust and the company’s reputation in the long term.
