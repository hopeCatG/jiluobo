Air Freight Services

Air freight is a method of transporting goods using airplanes. This mode of transport is extremely fast and efficient, ideal for high-priority or perishable goods, or for international shipments with tight deadlines. It offers advantages such as quick delivery times, shipment traceability, and the ability to reach global destinations rapidly and efficiently. However, air freight can be costly compared to other modes of transport, and cargo capacity is limited compared to road or rail transport. It is commonly used for valuable goods, high-tech products, medical samples, or urgent shipments. Its efficiency depends on well-developed airport infrastructure and dedicated logistics services.

Express is a rapid and priority shipping service ideal for high-priority, urgent, or international shipments that guarantee fast and reliable deliveries for goods or documents. It offers shipment tracking options, dedicated assistance, and door-to-door deliveries to ensure quick and reliable delivery times.

Groupage is a transportation method that combines multiple shipments from different senders into a single load, sharing the transport space to reduce costs and optimize space utilization.
