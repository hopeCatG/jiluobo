Land Freight Services

Land freight is a method of moving goods or people via roads, railways, or other land-based means. This type of transport is widely used for its versatility and ability to reach even remote destinations. It includes road transport of goods by truck, rail transport using freight and passenger trains, and rail transport of containerized goods. The efficiency and convenience of ground transport depend on the available infrastructure and transport networks, as well as specific regulations and logistical requirements of the transported cargo.

Our services

FTL (Full Truck Load) refers to a shipment that occupies the entire space of a truck without sharing the space with other goods. It is preferred for greater security and more precise delivery times compared to LTL (Less Than Truck Load) shipments.

LTL (Less Than Truck Load) refers to shipments that do not occupy the entire space of a truck and are transported together with other goods from different shippers. This shipping mode is suitable for smaller loads and offers advantages such as lower costs compared to FTL (Full Truck Load) and greater flexibility in logistics management.

Refrigerated transport involves moving temperature-sensitive goods, such as perishable foods or pharmaceuticals, using containers or vehicles with refrigeration systems. This ensures that the goods remain fresh and intact during transport, preserving their quality and safety along the distribution chain. It is essential to meet consumer needs and prevent food waste or loss of value.

Rail transport is a method of moving goods and passengers using trains on railway tracks. It is advantageous for long-distance transport, offering high load capacity, lower environmental impact, and greater safety. It is particularly suitable for bulk goods, containers, and heavy loads. Its efficiency depends on the railway infrastructure and the cooperation between railway operators and logistics companies.
