Integrated Policy for Environment, Safety, and Health

吉萝卜S.r.l. has adopted this Integrated Policy for Environment, Safety, and Health, which applies to all its operations, both in Italy and abroad, and to all its employees, directors, and collaborators. The Policy is based on the principles of sustainability, prevention, and continuous improvement.

1. Environmental Protection

The company is committed to minimizing its environmental impact through the efficient use of resources, the reduction of waste, and the prevention of pollution. It complies with all applicable environmental laws and regulations, and it implements best practices to protect the environment.

2. Occupational Safety and Health

The company is committed to ensuring a safe and healthy working environment for all employees. It identifies and assesses occupational risks, implements preventive measures, and provides training and information to all employees on safety and health matters.

3. Risk Management

The company has established a risk management system to identify, assess, and manage environmental, safety, and health risks. It regularly reviews and updates this system to ensure its effectiveness.

4. Compliance

The company complies with all applicable laws, regulations, and standards related to environment, safety, and health. It also adheres to the requirements of its customers and other stakeholders.

5. Continuous Improvement

The company is committed to continuous improvement in its environmental, safety, and health performance. It sets objectives and targets, monitors progress, and implements corrective actions when necessary.
